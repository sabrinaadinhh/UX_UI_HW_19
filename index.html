It’s a pleasure to meet you! My name is Sabrina Dinh, and I am a junior UX/UI Designer. I strive to strengthen my skills in user empathy and building interfaces to help solve user problems and make products easy to use for users. My skills include prototyping, user research, visual design, and most importantly, team work! Please don’t be scared to reach out to me!

My UX Skills

Prototyping

I enjoy prototyping, connecting pages to one another. I find it fascinating being able to find different transitions and animations to make the product work

User Research

Being able to go out and interview people is one of the most rewarding aspects of being a UX/UI Designer. Obtaining people’s answers and finding out how they view products is the best way to improve a product 

Visual Design

I love being able to view every product as an artwork. Offering my creativity and designing products is a challenge that is so rewarding when users enjoy the end result 

My Work

CallBack

For this project, I worked alongside with 4 other UX Designers to create an app/website named “CallBack”. The goal that we wanted to achieve for CallBack was to create a space an environment for UX/UI Designers to give and receive feedback on their portfolios.
